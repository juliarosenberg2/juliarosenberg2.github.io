A Short Biography of Julia

Julia was born in Middlebury, VT and has lived there her whole life. She has two sisters and two dogs. She went to elementary school in Cornwall, VT and middle and high school in Middlebury, VT. She now attends Tufts University in Medford, MA.

Julia enjoys outdoor activities, such as hiking, biking, skiing (both snow and water), and swimming. She has been active in team sports her whole life, and currently plays for the Tufts Women's Lacrosse team.

Julia's favorite food is pizza and her favorite color is red. If she could only watch one movie for the rest of her life, it would be Love Actually. She is very disappointed it is coming off of Netflix in October.
